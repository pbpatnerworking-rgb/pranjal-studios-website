Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026 | Effective Date: July 21, 2026

At PRANJAL STUDIOS (operated by Pranjal Tiwari), accessible from https://pranjalstudios.com, the privacy of our visitors and application users is one of our top priorities. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by PRANJAL STUDIOS and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at stdhelp.support@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

PRANJAL STUDIOS develops Android applications and games including Kids Learning Games, STDHELP Smart Study, Dimag Ka Dahi Quiz, Policy Connect Pro, ScanMaster AI, Bubble Galaxy Legends, and Coloring Games: Color & Paint.

Depending on the specific app you use, we may collect the following limited types of data:

  • Non-Personal Information: Device hardware model, operating system version, anonymous unique device identifiers, network state, and app usage diagnostics.
  • Crash Diagnostics: Anonymized stack traces and performance metrics collected via Google Firebase Crashlytics to fix bugs and improve performance.
  • User-Provided Feedback: If you contact us directly via email or our support forms, we receive your email address, name, device model, and message content.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected in the following ways:

  • To maintain, optimize, and update our applications and mobile games.
  • To ensure smooth 60fps performance across diverse Android screen resolutions.
  • To identify, diagnose, and resolve app crashes and technical bugs.
  • To process support requests, feature recommendations, and user feedback.
  • To comply with Google Play Developer Program Policies and regulatory laws.

3. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA Compliance)

We take child privacy with utmost seriousness. For applications specifically directed toward children, such as Kids Learning Games and Coloring Games: Color & Paint:

  • We do NOT knowingly collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) from children under the age of 13.
  • We do NOT use behavioral advertising, interest-based tracking, or social media plugins in child-directed apps.
  • Any advertisements displayed in family-directed applications adhere strictly to Google Play Families Policy and are served through certified child-directed ad SDKs.
  • Parents and guardians who believe their child has provided personal information to us may contact us immediately at stdhelp.support@gmail.com, and we will promptly remove such information from our records.

4. Third-Party Advertising & SDK Partners

Our applications may use Google-approved third-party service providers to display non-intrusive ads, analyze usage trends, or support in-app features. These third parties may collect anonymous device information subject to their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • AdMob by Google: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard administrative and technical safeguards to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Privacy Rights & Opt-Out

You have the right to opt-out of personalized advertising at any time through your Android device settings by navigating to Settings > Google > Ads > Reset Advertising ID / Opt out of Ads Personalization.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be published directly on this page with an updated "Effective Date". We encourage users to review this page periodically for any changes.
