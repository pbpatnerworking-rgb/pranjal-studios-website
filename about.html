PRANJAL STUDIOS

Building Software with Passion & Purpose

PRANJAL STUDIOS is an independent Android app and game development studio founded by Pranjal Tiwari. Driven by a passion for technology, education, and mobile UI design, our goal is to build software products that make learning effortless, study management seamless, and gaming entertaining.

From early childhood education tools like Kids Learning Games and comprehensive study aids like STDHELP Smart Study to viral brain teasers like Dimag Ka Dahi Quiz, we engineer every application with a commitment to speed, accessibility, zero unnecessary permissions, and full Google Play Console safety standards.

📚

Educational Applications

Designing interactive learning experiences for children and students to build foundational skills in phonics, numbers, and core academic subjects.

🚀

Productivity & AI Utilities

Developing lightweight utility tools like ScanMaster AI and Policy Connect Pro to streamline everyday digital documentation and task tracking.

🎮

Arcade & Casual Gaming

Crafting high-performance mobile games with vibrant visual aesthetics, fluid mechanics, and engaging offline gameplay support.
